World-Class Infrastructure

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to support holistic learning and development.

ICT Classrooms

Modern, technology-integrated classrooms equipped with smart boards, projectors and high-speed internet for interactive learning experiences.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern apparatus enabling hands-on experimentation and discovery-based learning.

Library

An extensive library stocked with thousands of books, periodicals, encyclopedias and digital resources to nurture a love of reading and research.

Sports Facilities

Large playgrounds, indoor sports areas, and facilities for cricket, football, basketball, and athletics to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Music & Arts

Dedicated spaces for music, art, and cultural activities — nurturing creativity, expression, and appreciation for the arts among students.

Counseling Center

Professional counselors available to support students' emotional, social, and academic wellbeing for a healthy and balanced school life.

Assembly Hall

A spacious assembly hall for morning prayers, cultural events, competitions, guest lectures, and all-school gatherings and celebrations.

Computer Lab

Modern computer labs with the latest hardware and software to ensure every student gains essential digital skills required in today's world.

Safe Transportation

Reliable and safe school transport services covering key areas of Jamalpur and surrounding regions for the convenience of students and parents.
