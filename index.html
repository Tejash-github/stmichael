Admission 2026–27

Documents Required

  1. Birth Certificate (Municipal Corporation or equivalent)
  2. Candidate's Aadhaar Card
  3. Parents' (Mother & Father) Aadhaar Card
  4. Residential Proof of Parents
  5. Caste Certificate (if SC/ST/OBC/EBC)
  6. Two passport-size photographs of the Candidate
  7. For Mont./LKG/UKG: two photos of child with parents
  8. Transfer Certificate & PEN from previous school (at time of admission)

Our Story

A Legacy of Learning & Service

St. Michael's High School, Jamalpur has been a beacon of quality education for decades. Situated in the heart of Jamalpur, Bihar, our institution is committed to the holistic development of every student — academically, morally, and socially.

Affiliated with CBSE, we offer a curriculum that blends traditional values with modern pedagogy, preparing students for the challenges of the 21st century. Our motto, "Ever to Love and to Serve", guides everything we do.
