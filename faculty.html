In Memoriam

Our Dedicated Souls

Honoring the visionaries and educators whose selfless devotion and legacy continue to inspire St. Michael's High School.

Mrs. Gladys Michael

1942 — 2019 Founder & First Principal

A dedicated NCC guide captain and teacher at NDA Jamalpur for nearly 20 years. Her profound passion for education and commitment to serving children led to the founding of St. Michael's High School in 1988.

Mr. Michael Francis

Passed away 2020 Co-Founder

Originally from Bettiah, he served selflessly in the accounts department of St. Michael's after retiring as a railway employee in 1990. A man of diverse talents who nurtured the school since its inception.

Miss Reena Michael

1968 — 2003 Vice-Principal (1995-2000)

Remembered for her valiancy, warmth, and dedication as an educator and administrator. Her untimely passing in 2003 left an enduring legacy of love and service in the hearts of the St. Michael's family.
